    • Style Tiles

    This is one of the steps in planning to design a website. Style Tiles are great for showing a client the color palette, typography, and imagery that will be used in the site. When you pair a style tile with a wire frame you can almost see what the final website might look like.

    The image above is a snapshot of the stile tile that most closely resembles the decisions I made regarding the Greenville Pediatric Dentistry site. In this stile tile you can see the lighter color pallete that I chose and how the colors were planned to layer on top of each other.
